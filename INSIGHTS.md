# HR Analytics Insights

## Key Findings

- Overall attrition rate is significant, indicating potential employee dissatisfaction.
- Sales and Marketing departments show higher attrition compared to others.
- Employees with lower experience (1–3 years) are more likely to leave.
- Higher salaries correlate with better retention.
- IT and Finance departments have higher average salaries.
- Performance rating does not always align with salary, indicating possible pay gaps.

## Recommendations

- Improve retention strategies for Sales & Marketing teams.
- Introduce salary adjustments for high-performing employees.
- Focus on employee engagement for early-career employees.
- Use KPI dashboards for continuous monitoring.
